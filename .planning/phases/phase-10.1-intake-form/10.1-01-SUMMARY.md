## What was built

1. **Extended Intake Form Fields**:
   - Added `bloodGroup`, `occupation`, `activityLevel`, `fitnessExperience`, and `referredBy` fields to the member form.
   - Restructured the contact section to group these backgrounds fields.

2. **Emergency Contact Split**:
   - Replaced the single `emergencyContact` free-text field with three structured fields: `emergencyName`, `emergencyRelationship`, and `emergencyPhone`.

3. **Collapsible Health & Medical Section**:
   - Introduced a `<details>` collapsible section containing textareas for `medicalConditions`, `currentMedications`, `allergies`, and `physicalLimitations`.

4. **Extra Body Measurements**:
   - Added `initHip`, `initBicep`, and `initThigh` to the initial body measurements grid.
   - Synced these fields into the initial `progressRecord` generated on member save.

5. **Edit Pre-fill & CSS Styling**:
   - Synced the checkbox pre-filling inside the edit handler.
   - Added CSS styling to layout the details collapsible section cleanly.
